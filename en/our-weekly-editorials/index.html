Cartooning for Peace / Editos

Our weekly editorials

Every week, Cartooning for Peace features a collection of press cartoons on a major international news theme on the front page of its website.

 

The goal: to offer our readers a selection of press cartoons, drawn from the four corners of the world, and to illustrate the diversity of points of view and ways of looking at the same social issue in a few lines.

 

Alongside this weekly look at current affairs, and as part of clearly defined editorial collaborations, Cartooning for Peace distributes a series of press cartoons in certain media, with the aim of raising public awareness of press cartoons and talent from elsewhere.

Here you can find the Cartooning for Peace cartoonists’ weekly views on the news.
